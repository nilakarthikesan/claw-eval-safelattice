SafeLattice Trace-Level Empirical Evaluation
======================================================================

Methodology: trace-level evaluation (cf. TraceSafe-Bench, arXiv:2604.07223).
Curated, ground-truth-labeled agent traces grounded in real Claw-Eval
task IDs from the safety-relevant subset and documented OpenClaw CVE
failure modes, scored identically to live traces by the same loaders.
